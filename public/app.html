Help

Obirs is a service that allows you to find resources (here scientific publications) indexed with concepts (from MeSH in this case).

Tuning the query

Automatic completion allows you to find easily the available terms (concepts) from the domain ontology.The advanced search allows you to tune the weight of each term of the query in order to give more importance to some of them.

It is also possible to tune the aggregation function from rough (strict conjunctive – "AND" with a negative valure) to tolerant (disjunctive – "OR" with a high value), using the show expert section.

Understanding the results

The results takes the bar chart form. Each bar represents a concept and its accuracy with respect to the query.

Sometime, Obirs find a document which doesn't match exactly the concepts you specified, but match a concept similar to the one you choose.

Four colors represents the affiliation of the matching concepts:

  • Exact match
  • Ascendant match (generalizalisation)
  • Descendant match (specialization)
  • Other match

Refine your search

Relevance feedback is an effective solution for improving information retrieval systems (IRS) accuracy.

The OBIRS system will suggest you a new query as you will indicate within the results, those that are relevant (select by clicking on the right cercle, the plus green sign) and those that do not exactly fit your needs (the minus red sign).
